## CONTENT BLOCK 1 — Rich Text: Services Overview

Oil City Ready Mix Ltd. offers three core concrete service categories:

**1. Civil & Municipal Concrete**
Engineered mixes for roads, bridges, drainage, and public infrastructure.
- High-strength mixes up to 45 MPa
- Large-volume capacity up to 1,000 m³
- Compliant with all municipal and provincial standards
- Suitable for: roads, bridges, curbs, sidewalks, storm drainage

**2. Commercial Concrete**
Reliable ready mix supply for warehouses, retail centers, offices, and mixed-use developments.
- Flexible scheduling to match your project timeline
- Tailored structural mix designs per engineer specifications
- Consistent batching quality on every load
- Suitable for: slabs, footings, columns, parking lots, industrial floors

**3. Residential Concrete**
Quality concrete for home builders and residential contractors.
- Minimum order: 1 m³
- Mix strengths: 20–40 MPa for residential applications
- Timely delivery across the Edmonton region
- Suitable for: foundations, driveways, patios, walkways, garage floors, basement slabs

## CONTENT BLOCK 5 — FAQ: Services & Product Questions

**Q: What is the minimum order size?**
A: Our minimum order is 1 cubic metre (m³). Whether you're doing a small residential slab or a large commercial foundation, we can accommodate your project.

**Q: What is the maximum order size?**
A: We can handle up to 1,000 m³. Our fleet includes batch mixers and trailers of all sizes to support large-scale civil and commercial pours.

**Q: What concrete strengths do you offer?**
A: We supply concrete from 20 MPa to 45 MPa. Residential projects typically use 20–40 MPa; commercial and civil projects may require up to 45 MPa.

**Q: Do you work on residential projects?**
A: Yes. We serve residential builders and homeowners for foundations, driveways, patios, walkways, and garage slabs. Minimum order is 1 m³.

**Q: Do you work on commercial and civil projects?**
A: Yes. We supply concrete for commercial buildings, warehouses, retail centers, municipal infrastructure, roads, and bridges, up to 1,000 m³.

**Q: What information do I need to provide for a quote?**
A: Project type (residential, commercial, or civil), required concrete strength (MPa), volume needed (m³), delivery address, and preferred pour date.

**Q: How quickly can you deliver?**
A: Delivery timelines depend on availability and project size. Contact us at 780-318-4949 to confirm scheduling.
