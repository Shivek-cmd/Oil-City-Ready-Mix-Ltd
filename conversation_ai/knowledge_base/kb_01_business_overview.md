## CONTENT BLOCK 1 — Rich Text: About the Business

Oil City Ready Mix Ltd. (OCRM) is an emerging and trusted ready mix concrete supplier serving the Edmonton, Alberta region. We supply high-quality concrete for civil, commercial, and residential construction projects, tailored to each client's needs.

Our goal is to be a leading provider of high-quality concrete solutions in the Edmonton region. In a short span of time, we've become a trusted supplier for builders and contractors. We prioritize safety, quality, and strict adherence to industry standards, ensuring every job is completed with precision and care. With competitive pricing and a customer-focused approach, we work closely with our customers to meet their project requirements, timelines, and overall expectations.

## CONTENT BLOCK 3 — Rich Text: Corporate Structure

Oil City Ready Mix Ltd. is a 100% wholly owned subsidiary of **Oil City Holding Ltd. (OCHL)**.

OCHL provides strategic oversight, governance, financial stability, and operational direction across all entities. All companies maintain operational independence while benefiting from shared resources and centralized management.

Other subsidiaries under OCHL:
- **Oil City Property Management Ltd. (OCPM)** — Property Management
- **Oil City Trucking Ltd. (OCT)** — Trucking
